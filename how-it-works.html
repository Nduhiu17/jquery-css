How Stokisha Works.

1. Register for an account. If you already have an account with us simply login.

2. Register your business details and if your are a Retailer or Distributor, fill in your business details and submit for approval

3. Login to the Stokisha App and order for goods.

4. Stokisha pays the supplier through their paybill.

Expansion

We are in Kenya's capital Nairobi and nearby peri-urban settlements such as Machakos, Kajiado and Kiambu counties to list a few.

We intend to expand to other major towns such as Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret and Garissa in the near future. We also plan to move out of Kenya into the greater East African region.
